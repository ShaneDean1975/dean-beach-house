Welcome

Your Home on the Gulf

Affectionately known as "Jubilee," the Dean Family Beach House is a relaxed, family-friendly retreat just steps from the white sand of Fort Morgan, with a gorgeous Gulf view as your backdrop. It's tastefully, nautically decorated with antique and vintage finds from the area — and furnished with everything you need for a great beach vacation.

The home has two decks — one covered, one partially covered — each with plenty of chairs for soaking up the breeze. We furnish beach chairs and a small cart to roll them down to the water, plus a picnic table under the house, a high table for cleaning your catch, and a completely enclosed outdoor shower for rinsing off the sand.

Comfortably sleeping up to 10 guests across three bedrooms, it's a true home away from home — a fully stocked kitchen, comfortable beds, beach gear, family games, and a TV in every room. Fort Morgan offers the quiet, uncrowded side of Alabama's Gulf Coast, yet you're a short drive from the shops and restaurants of Gulf Shores. Whether it's a week in the summer or a longer winter getaway, this is a place to slow down and make memories.

Getting Here

The Fort Morgan peninsula sits at the western tip of Alabama's Gulf Coast — a peaceful, uncrowded stretch of beach about 22 miles west of Gulf Shores.

  • Gulf Shores~30 min
  • Orange Beach~45 min
  • Pensacola Airport (PNS)~1 hr 15 min
  • Mobile, AL~1 hr 15 min

Nearby: Fort Morgan Historic Site, Mobile Bay Ferry, fresh seafood markets, charter fishing, and miles of quiet beach.
