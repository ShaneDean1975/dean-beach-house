Welcome

Your Home on the Gulf

Tucked right on the white sand of Fort Morgan, the Dean Family Beach House is a relaxed, family-friendly retreat where the Gulf of Mexico is your front yard. Wake up to sunrise over the water, spend your days with toes in the sand, and gather each evening on the deck to watch the sun melt into the horizon.

This beachfront home comfortably sleeps up to 10 guests across four bedrooms, with an open living and dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and wide Gulf-facing decks made for long mornings with coffee and slow evenings with friends.

Fort Morgan offers the quiet, uncrowded side of Alabama's Gulf Coast — yet you're a short drive from the shops, restaurants, and attractions of Gulf Shores and Orange Beach. Whether it's a week in the summer or a longer winter getaway, this is a place to slow down and make memories.

Getting Here

The Fort Morgan peninsula sits at the western tip of Alabama's Gulf Coast — a peaceful, uncrowded stretch of beach about 22 miles west of Gulf Shores.

  • Gulf Shores~30 min
  • Orange Beach~45 min
  • Pensacola Airport (PNS)~1 hr 15 min
  • Mobile, AL~1 hr 15 min

Nearby: Fort Morgan Historic Site, Mobile Bay Ferry, fresh seafood markets, charter fishing, and miles of quiet beach.
